Who I am

My name is Kathia Urdiaín. I’m a psychologist with a master’s degree in Solution‑Focused Brief Therapy and over ten years of experience helping people overcome anxiety, depression, work stress and relationship issues. Having trained in both the U.S. and Mexico, I understand the cultural nuances of both countries.

As a mental health professional, I focus on creating a safe, confidential and empathetic space. I firmly believe that we all have the capacity for change and that, with the right tools, we can overcome our challenges.

My approach

I work with techniques from brief therapy, cognitive‑behavioral therapy and mindfulness. Each treatment plan is personalized based on your goals and your story.

My mission

To accompany individuals and couples on their journey toward emotional wellbeing in a practical and effective way. My aim is for you to feel supported and equipped with useful tools from the very first session.
