Trauma & PTSD: hope and recovery

Traumatic experiences can leave deep marks on your mind and body. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is characterized by intrusive memories, avoidance and constant hypervigilance.

Common symptoms

  • Reliving the traumatic event through flashbacks or nightmares.
  • Avoiding places, people or activities that remind you of the trauma.
  • Persistent anxiety or irritability.
  • Trouble sleeping or relaxing.
  • Feelings of guilt or shame related to the event.

How brief therapy helps

Brief therapy offers a safe space to process trauma and learn emotional regulation techniques. We use gradual exposure and resilience-building strategies to help you regain control and trust.
