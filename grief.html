Grief & loss: healing with support

Losing a loved one or going through a major change can trigger intense emotions. Healthy grieving allows you to honor the loss and move forward while keeping your memories alive.

Common symptoms

  • Deep sadness and frequent crying.
  • Feelings of guilt or regret.
  • Lack of energy or apathy.
  • Difficulty accepting the loss.
  • Withdrawal from social contact.

How brief therapy helps

Through brief therapy we work on accepting the pain, expressing your emotions and finding ways to remember your loved one in a healthy way. We also develop skills to adapt to your new reality.
