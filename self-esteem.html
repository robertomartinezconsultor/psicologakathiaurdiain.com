Self-esteem & self-concept: reclaim your worth

Low self-esteem shows up as negative self-talk and a lack of confidence when making decisions. It can affect your relationships and overall wellbeing.

Signs of low self-esteem

  • You constantly criticize yourself.
  • You find it hard to accept compliments or acknowledge achievements.
  • You feel you're not enough.
  • You avoid new challenges for fear of failure.
  • You depend on external approval.

How brief therapy helps

Through brief therapy we identify your strengths, replace self-critical thoughts with a positive self-image and cultivate habits of self-compassion.

Immediate exercise

Write down three things you value about yourself today. This simple practice can start to shift your self-perception.
