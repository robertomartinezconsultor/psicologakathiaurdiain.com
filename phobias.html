Phobias & fears: facing and overcoming them

Specific phobias, such as fear of flying, heights or confined spaces, trigger intense reactions even though the danger is minimal. Avoiding these situations reduces your quality of life.

Common symptoms

  • Extreme fear or panic around a specific object or situation.
  • Sweating, palpitations or shaking.
  • Avoiding places or activities that trigger the fear.
  • A sense of losing control.
  • Trouble breathing or dizziness.

How brief therapy helps

Through gradual exposure and cognitive restructuring, brief therapy helps you change the fear response and regain confidence to face the feared situation.

Immediate tip

Identify the irrational thought behind your fear and replace it with a more realistic one. Practice deep breathing to ease physical symptoms.
