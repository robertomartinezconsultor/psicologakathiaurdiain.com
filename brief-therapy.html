What is brief therapy?

Solution-focused brief therapy is designed to provide practical tools for emotional problems within just 8–12 sessions. Instead of months of exploring your past, we work on resources you already have to feel better now.

This modality works well for anxiety, depression, stress, grief and relationship issues. Sessions are usually one hour long and end with clear strategies you can apply right away.

Benefits of brief therapy

  • Tangible results within a few weeks.
  • Flexible structure: sessions adapt to your pace and needs.
  • Goal-oriented: each session ends with practical steps.
  • Ideal for people with busy schedules or who want quick solutions.

What happens in a session?

During the first session we explore your current situation and define what changes you want to achieve. In later sessions we review progress, adjust strategies and practice techniques you can use in daily life.

Who is it for?

Brief therapy is suitable for adults, teens and couples who need immediate relief without a long therapeutic process. If you’re unsure whether this approach fits you, feel free to reach out.
