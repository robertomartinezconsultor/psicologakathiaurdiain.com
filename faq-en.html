Frequently asked questions

What happens in the first session?

In your initial session we explore your current situation and goals. There are no instant diagnoses or judgments; it’s a space for listening and planning.

How many sessions will I need?

Brief therapy typically lasts between 8 and 12 sessions, though it may vary depending on your progress and needs.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes. Everything shared in session is private and confidentiality is respected according to U.S. and Mexican privacy laws.

Can I come even if I don’t have a severe diagnosis?

Absolutely. Anyone seeking to improve their emotional well-being can benefit from brief therapy, regardless of symptom severity.

Do you offer online therapy?

Yes. I provide secure video sessions for people in San Antonio, Mexico and beyond.
