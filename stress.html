Stress & Burnout: signs and solutions

Chronic stress and occupational burnout take a toll on your physical and mental health. They often appear as constant fatigue, irritability and difficulty concentrating.

Common symptoms

  • Persistent fatigue and lack of energy.
  • Muscle tension, headaches or stomach problems.
  • Difficulty disconnecting from work.
  • A sense of detachment or cynicism towards responsibilities.
  • Decreased productivity and motivation.

How brief therapy helps

Brief therapy teaches stress-management techniques and relaxation strategies, as well as healthy boundary setting and time management. With professional support, you can prevent burnout and regain balance.
